Course We Offer

We are offer to three best computer science course for student at Vardhaman college

B.Tech CSE

The Bachelor of Technology in Computer Science and Engineering is a 4-year undergraduate program that focuses on computer programming, algorithms, software development, and hardware systems.

MCA

The Master of Computer Applications is a 2-year postgraduate course that provides in-depth knowledge of computer applications, software engineering, and data analytics to prepare students for IT and software industries.

BSc Computer Science

The BSc in Computer Science is a 3-year degree that emphasizes the theoretical and practical foundations of computing, programming, data structures, artificial intelligence, and operating systems.
